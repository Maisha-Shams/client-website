What intrigues me to do what I do

A photographer doesn’t need expensive equipment to take great photos. The best camera gear in the world is not going to help a photographer see or be aware of his or her surroundings. Light is probably the most important tool. The way the nature of light affects the way things are seen intrigues me the most. Intensity, direction, and type of light offer a potential for visual exploration. The transition from the afternoon golden hour to the twilight dusk is really a treat to us photographers. Then there are moments when you get to capture moments and emotions forever in a single frame to be cherished forever.
